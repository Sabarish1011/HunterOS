Opportunity Score

Components:

1. Value Gap Score
2. Demand Score
3. Liquidity Score
4. Distress Score
5. Arbitrage Score
6. Risk Score

Final Formula:

Opportunity Score =

(0.25 × ValueGap)
+
(0.20 × Demand)
+
(0.15 × Liquidity)
+
(0.15 × Distress)
+
(0.15 × Arbitrage)
+
(0.10 × (100 - Risk))

Outputs:

0-39     Ignore
40-69    Watch
70-84    Investigate
85-94    High Opportunity
95-100   Immediate Action
